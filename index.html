About Us

Mr. Gujarathi Sathish Kumar, an International certified yoga trainer is the founder of DasYogashala (Established in 2020) in Hyderabad.

Mr.Sathish kumar is extremely adept Yoga instructor with ample experience and training in Yoga which is fuelled by his passion for the role of spreading the teachings of yoga.

He has completed the RYT 200 Hrs program, certified by the Kalpataa yoga Institute.

His expertise consists of Traditional Hatha Yoga, Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga and Meditation.
